Static Top Navbar Example

This example page shows off how a top-aligned navbar and its responsive behaviors. This navbar will remain in its original position and moves with the rest of the page as you scroll.

In order to experience the responsive behavior of the navbar, you will have to resize your browser above and below the lg breakpoint.
