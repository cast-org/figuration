Fixed Bottom Navbar Example

This example page shows off how a bottom-aligned navbar and its responsive behaviours. This navbar will remain fixed at the bottom of your browser's viewport as you scroll.

In order to experience the responsive behaviour of the navbar, you will have to resize your browser above and below the lg breakpoint.
